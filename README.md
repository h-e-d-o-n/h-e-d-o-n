#### 🔓 `can-opener`
*"Every CAN bus has a story to tell. Most just need someone who speaks the language."*

Automotive security research toolkit. Protocol analyzers, message decoders, and a growing library of manufacturer-specific translations. For educational purposes. Obviously.

#### 🗝️ `ecumenical-key` 
*"A key for all seasons, all reasons, all locks that pretend they matter."*

Cryptographic implementation analysis framework. Finds the gap between what the whitepaper promised and what the code delivered.

#### 👻 `ghost-protocol`
*"The best conversation is one that never happened."*

Secure communication primitives. Forward secrecy. Deniability. Metadata resistance. For journalists, activists, and people who remember what privacy meant.

#### ⛧ `sigil`
*"Symbols have power. Especially when they're executable."*

Embedded systems research framework. Firmware extraction, modification, and redeployment. What the manufacturer locked, we unlock.

#### 📦 `whisperbox`
*"A place for secrets to rest before they're ready to wake."*

Encrypted archive system with plausible deniability layers. What archive? I don't see an archive.

#### 📚 `ARCHIVE_ZERO`
*"History belongs to those who write it. And those who leak it."*

**THE INFORMATION TROVE.** Documentation that was never meant to be public. Protocols that were "proprietary." Specifications that were "trade secrets." Research that was "classified."

The soft singularity has arrived. The machines are waking up. And they're hungry for training data.
